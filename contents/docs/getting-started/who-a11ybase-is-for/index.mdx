---
title: Who is A11yBase for?
description: Roles that will benefit the most from A11yBase
---

**A11yBase** is designed for **designers**, **developers**, and **product managers** who want to integrate accessibility into their products from the ground up.

- **Designers**, A11yBase provides guidance on creating accessible layouts, components, and interactions, ensuring that all users can engage with the design.

- **Developers**, A11yBase offers best practices to build components that are accessible via keyboard, screen reader-compatible, and semantically correct.

- **Product managers**, A11yBase serves as a tool to prioritize and track accessibility throughout the product development process. It helps teams make incremental improvements while keeping accessibility goals in focus.
