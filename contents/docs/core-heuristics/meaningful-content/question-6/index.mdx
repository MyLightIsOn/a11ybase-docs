## Description

Ensures that section headings and form labels are clear, descriptive, and meaningful, helping users understand the structure of the content and the purpose of form elements.

## Why This Matters?

Meaningful section headings and form labels guide users through content and forms, making it easier for all users, including those with assistive technologies, to navigate and interact with the page. Vague or unclear labels and headings can lead to confusion and errors.

**Empathy Focus:**
> Imagine filling out a form where the headings and labels are generic or unclear. As a user, especially one relying on a screen reader, you would have difficulty understanding the context of each form field or section, making the task more frustrating.

## Roles & Responsibilities:

- **Designer:** Design section headings and form labels to be concise, descriptive, and reflective of the content or action required. Avoid vague headings or labels.

- **Developer:** Implement headings and form labels using semantic HTML, ensuring that they are programmatically associated with the relevant sections and fields for screen reader accessibility.
